ABOUT US!

At Maharlika Food Street, we're more than just a restaurant, we're an experience. We're a gathering place where friends, families, and communities come together to savor the flavors of tradition. Our halal offerings are a celebration of Filipino cuisine, crafted with fresh ingredients and seasoned with love.

Join us at Maharlika Food Street, and let us take you on a culinary adventure to the heart of the Philippines. We're not just serving food, we're serving a taste of home.


We're proud to be a part of Taguig's vibrant tapestry, echoing the spirit of the "Maharlika." Just like these courageous warriors of old, we're bold in our flavors and passionate about sharing our heritage. Every dish we create tells a story, whispering tales of family recipes passed down through generations.

Food is our language, and we speak it fluently. From the sizzling sizzle of our sizzling sisig to the comforting warmth of our biryani, we pour our hearts into every plate. We believe that every bite should be a journey, a testament to the rich legacy of Filipino cuisine.

We promise to serve you not just food, but a feeling. Whether you're craving the familiar comfort of a home-cooked meal or seeking to discover new culinary adventures, we have something for everyone. We want you to leave our table feeling satisfied, connected, and brimming with the warmth of Filipino hospitality.
